Our History

A Legacy of Excellence in Architectural Visualization Founded by Mr. Sudhansu Sekhar Parhi (Baba), CADDHAWK began as a passion for architectural detailing and precision. With over 17 years of experience, Baba has built strong global connections with European architects and consulting engineers. Over the years, we have evolved into a trusted partner for professionals seeking cutting-edge visualization and CAD solutions.

Our Mission

At CADDHAWK, our mission is to deliver exceptional 3D services, CAD solutions, and innovative design support to our clients worldwide. We are committed to excellence, precision, and fostering long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

We envision becoming a global leader in architectural visualization and CAD services, recognized for our quality, reliability, and customer-centric approach.
